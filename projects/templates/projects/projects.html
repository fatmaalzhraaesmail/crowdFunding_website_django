The help Foundation seeks to achieve the implementation of a number of activities and programs to raise the efficiency of infrastructure projects and public services provided to citizens of the governorates, including: Implementation of hygiene programs, dealing with solid waste, beautifying squares and entrances to villages and cities, and improving the environment Extending electricity networks to disadvantaged and emerging areas and improving electricity services. Implementation of road paving projects to connect villages to each other and between cities within the governorate. Implementation of bridges and tunnels projects for cars and pedestrians. Implementation of firefighting, security and traffic projects, and provision of equipment and vehicles required for this program. Supporting the needs of local units. Completion of planned projects in poor villages.
